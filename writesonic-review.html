Writesonic Review – Is It Worth It?

Writesonic is an AI copywriting platform that helps you produce SEO‑friendly blog posts, ads, emails and more with remarkable speed and quality. It uniquely blends AI writing with built‑in SEO optimization to ensure your content ranks well and resonates with readers.

Start Free Trial

Key Features

AI Article Writer

Generate long‑form drafts with options to set word count, tone and add smart internal linking. The Article Writer 6.0 analyses SERPs and suggests length and structure to outrank competitors.

SEO Checker & Optimizer

Writesonic’s SEO module analyses your draft, compares it with ranking pages and provides keyword suggestions and long‑tail ideas . One‑click rewrites instantly boost your content score.

Versatile Templates

Create copy for blogs, ads, social media, product descriptions and even ebooks. Templates cover nearly every content format, making Writesonic a true all‑rounder.

No‑Code Chatbot & Extras

Beyond writing, Writesonic includes a no‑code AI chatbot builder, image generator, paraphrasing and rewriting tools and copy for Amazon listings.

Pricing

Writesonic offers a generous free plan (10,000 words/month), an Unlimited plan at $16/month, a Business plan at $12.67/month for 200k words (GPT‑3.5) and an Enterprise plan starting at $1,000/month. Annual billing discounts are available. Overall, the pricing is competitive compared to other AI writers.

Pros & Cons

Pros

• Easy‑to‑use interface with in‑editor optimisation.
• Real‑time SEO suggestions and competitor intel.
• Wide range of templates and extra tools.
• Affordable pricing with a generous free plan.

Cons

• Content can feel robotic and needs human editing.
• Long‑form output may require deeper research for complex topics .
• Some advanced SEO features depend on higher tiers.

Verdict

Writesonic is one of the most feature‑rich AI writing tools on the market. For marketers, bloggers and small businesses it provides an affordable way to produce high‑quality content quickly. While it isn’t perfect—AI‑generated copy still requires human editing—it offers tremendous value when paired with a thoughtful content strategy.
